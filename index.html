About RoboTech

We offer structured and interesting courses designed by IIT-IIM and PhD professors.

If you are looking for some activities to engage your child from 6 years old, we have a solution. Robotics is an exciting hobby that can influence your child's future career choices. Robotics develops thinking, fine motor skills and logic, teaches children to understand the technology, and generally increases their creativity.

We've designed fun, practice-filled activities for your kids aged 6 to 16. Sign up for a free trial lesson, and your kid will love robotics!
